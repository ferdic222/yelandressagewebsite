Respiration & skin

Inhalation

We maintain lung and skin wellness with our inhalation system. Respiratory health and skin condition are supported as part of routine care for the high-performance horse—not as a medical fix, but as ongoing wellness.

The inhalation machine is integrated into our horse wellness programme so that horses in heavy training can breathe well and stay in optimal condition. For more details and to book, please get in touch.
